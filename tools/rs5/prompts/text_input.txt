with flat vector art. Characters (chibi-style, ~200px tall) interact in scenes by walking,
touching objects, and moving through doors to other scenes.

layout for this space as a 2D side-view game scene, and produce a scene template in JSON format.

{schema}

Rules:

- Object zones describe areas, not specific objects.
